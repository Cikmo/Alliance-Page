While sailing through the skies, dreaming of the future bright, the Divine Phoenix was having the time of its life. But suddenly the dreams of the Phoenix turned into nightmares which the Divine Phoenix couldn't control. Flying controlless in the dark skies it soon dumbled into the grounds of Cataclysmic waste. Laying lifeless on the ground, soon the power of Cataclysm took over the corpse.

But deep inside the remains of the Divine Phoenix was life, life of the old Sanghelios, who we're preparing to take over what's left to take. Soon after the crueling war in the mist of the Cataclysmic aura, The New Sanghelios sprouted. Breaking away from the Cataclysm power, finding shelter under the strong shoulders of Carthago, a new power was born, The New Sanghelios was born.

Watching back to the tales of the Divine Phoenix and kind people of the Cataclysm, we decided to go, go the different way and fight to the top ourselves, wishing to get the old Sanghelios back.
